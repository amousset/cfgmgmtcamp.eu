Jerakia Day

6th February 2019

Jerakia Day

Please join us on February 6th for the first ever Jerakia Day!

This will be your chance to meet other Jerakia devs and users, learn more about who they are and what they do, and hopefully do some work together on the project!

To get a feeling for what we could be up to, please browse through the projects in the new Jerkia github organization and see if you find any interesting bits.

  • Jerakia server
  • Jerkia clients, client libraries
  • Work on our in-progress integrations with Ansible, Terraform
  • Discuss use cases
  • Bugs, features, maybe even pull requests?

Room and other details TBA, check back on this page later!

Please remember to register that you want to join us through the general Config Management Camp registration system.
